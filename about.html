About Elina Events Co.

We are dedicated to orchestrating flawless and memorable events, crafted with precision and passion.

Our Story & Philosophy

Founded with a vision to redefine event experiences, Elina Events Co. has grown into a leading name in sophisticated event management. Our philosophy centers on understanding your unique aspirations and transforming them into tangible, breathtaking realities. We believe that every celebration, whether grand or intimate, deserves meticulous attention to detail and a touch of extraordinary.

Our journey began with a passion for design, impeccable planning, and a commitment to client satisfaction. Today, we continue to uphold these values, ensuring that each event we undertake is executed flawlessly, leaving a lasting impression on you and your guests.

Meet Our Founder

Behind every successful event is our founder Ratna Dahivelkar. Ratna Dahivelkar is a seasoned anchor and voice-over artist with over 15 years of experience in radio, television, and stage events. Associated with All India Radio and Doordarshan Sahyadri, she is known for her graceful presence and powerful voice. As the founder of Elina Events, she curates and hosts musical shows, weddings, corporate events, and cultural programs across Maharashtra. A mentor at heart, Ratna also conducts anchoring workshops to inspire the next generation of presenters

Just remember that the professionals working for Elina Events are not just event managers; we are dream weavers, dedicated to making your special moments truly shine.
